Visualizing Spdy and Quic Packets

A website visualizing the data we have profiled comparing top 200 Alexa ranked sites. To demonstrate this we have graphed comparisons of Spdy and Quic packets vs HTTP/1.1 packets. We have several graphs as seen below each portraying different types of comparisons

Timeline

A graph portraying the timeline of all GET requests from most of the top 200 alex sites. Spdy+Quic sites are green, Spdy sites are yellow and HTTP sites are red.

CDF

A graph portraying the cumulative distribution function of page load times. Spdy+Quic sites are green, Spdy sites are yellow and HTTP sites are red.
